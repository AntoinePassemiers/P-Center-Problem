The p-center instances have the following structure 

n p
d11 d12 ... d1n
d21 d22 ... d2n
 .   . .     .
 .   .   .   .
 .   .     . .
dn1 dn2 ... dnn

where n is the number of possible sites, p is the maximum number of centers to open and d is the matrix of distances between site i and j.

The folder 'easy' contains randomly generated instances of small size. 
The folder 'hard' contains more challenging instances taken from OR-Library.
